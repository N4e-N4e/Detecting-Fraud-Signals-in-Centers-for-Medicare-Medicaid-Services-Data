### Description:

Medicare Durable Medical Equipment, Devices & Supplies - by Supplier

The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) has prepared a pair of public data sets, the Provider Utilization and Payment Data Durable Medical Equipment, Devices & Supplies Datasets, with information on DMEPOS products and services provided to Medicare beneficiaries ordered by physicians and other healthcare professionals. The Durable Medical Equipment, Devices & Supplies Dataset contains information on utilization, payment (allowed amount and Medicare payment), and submitted charges organized by National Provider Identifier (NPI), Healthcare Common Procedure Coding System (HCPCS) code, and supplier rental indicator. The data in the Medicare Durable Medical Equipment, Devices & Supplies dataset contains 100% final-action (i.e., all claim adjustments have been resolved) Part B non-institutional DMEPOS line items for the Medicare fee-for-service (FFS) population. This product includes separately aggregated files by referring and supplying provider NPI.
